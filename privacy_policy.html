Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Introduction

"Are You Alive?" is a simple check-in app that reminds you to open it periodically. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

The app collects and stores the following information:

  • Your Name: When you first open the app, you are asked to enter your name. This is used solely to personalize the message displayed on the home screen.

How Your Information is Stored

All information is stored locally on your device only. We do not have servers, and your data never leaves your device. Specifically:

  • Your name is stored in local device storage (SharedPreferences on Android)
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No data is shared with any third parties
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used

Notifications

The app uses local notifications to remind you to check in if you haven't opened the app in a while. These notifications:

  • Are scheduled and triggered entirely on your device
  • Do not require an internet connection
  • Can be disabled through your device's notification settings

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you periodic check-in reminders

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Uninstall the app, or
  • Clear the app's data through your device settings

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
